Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a recording?

Open the app, tap Record meeting or Post-call note, then press Start. Recording begins only when you tap the button.

Why is the microphone permission required?

Call Recorder Automatic uses your microphone to capture audio. Grant microphone access when prompted so you can record meetings and voice notes.

Where are my recordings stored?

All recordings are saved locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers unless you choose to share them using the Share button.

Can I change the app language?

Yes. On the home screen, tap the globe icon to switch between French, English, Arabic, and Spanish.

Does the app record phone calls automatically?

No. Call Recorder Automatic does not automatically record iPhone cellular calls. Use it to manually save voice notes and summaries after a call, with full consent from everyone involved.
